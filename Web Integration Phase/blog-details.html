EV Insights

Visualization Tool for Electric Vehicle Charge & Range Analysis

  • Project Team
  • 2025

Electric vehicles generate massive amounts of data related to battery health, energy consumption, driving behavior, and charging efficiency. Visualization tools transform this raw data into meaningful insights.

Our EV Charge & Range Analysis platform uses interactive dashboards to help users understand real-time battery levels, predicted driving range, and energy efficiency under different conditions.

“Effective visualization reduces range anxiety and improves confidence in electric mobility.”

Why Visualization Matters

  • Real-time battery monitoring
  • Accurate range prediction
  • Energy consumption comparison
  • Better trip planning

By combining Tableau dashboards, structured datasets, and web technologies, this project bridges the gap between raw EV data and user decision-making.
